Frequently Asked Questions

Is GHP Cloner safe to use?

Yes, GHP Cloner is completely safe. It runs locally on your device and doesn't store or share your Discord token. All cloning operations are performed directly through Discord's API.

What permissions do I need to clone a server?

You need to be an administrator on both the source and target servers. The bot requires the "Manage Server" permission to create roles and channels.

How long does the cloning process take?

The cloning process typically takes 1-2 minutes, depending on the size of the server. Larger servers with many roles and channels may take slightly longer.

Can I clone server emojis and stickers?

Currently, GHP Cloner focuses on cloning roles and channels. Emoji and sticker cloning will be added in a future update.

How do I get a license key?

License keys can be purchased through our Discord server. Join our community to get access to the latest versions and support.

About GHP Cloner

Developed by GhostGamer (@ghstgmr)

GHP Cloner was created to solve the problem of manually recreating Discord server structures. As an experienced Discord server administrator, I found the process of setting up new servers time-consuming and repetitive.

This tool automates the process of cloning server roles, channels, and permissions, saving server admins hours of work. All operations are performed locally on your device for maximum security.
